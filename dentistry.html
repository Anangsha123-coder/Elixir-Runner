Common Dental Problems

Understanding common dental problems can help in taking better care of your oral health. Here are some issues patients may encounter:

Cavities

Tooth decay caused by bacterial build-up that creates holes in teeth. Requires fillings or other treatment.

Gingivitis

Inflammation of the gums, often due to poor oral hygiene. Can lead to gum disease if untreated.

Toothache

Pain in or around the tooth, which could be a sign of infection or other issues.

Bad Breath

Caused by bacteria, gum disease, or other dental issues. Regular cleaning and treatment can help.

Sensitivity

Sharp pain when consuming hot, cold, or sugary foods. Often related to enamel wear or gum recession.

Bruxism

Teeth grinding, often during sleep, that can lead to tooth damage and jaw pain.
